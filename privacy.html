Privacy

Agent Console does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. There is no backend of ours in the middle.

The app talks only to the agent hosts you configure, over connections you set up — direct WebSocket or a cloud tunnel you control. Your messages, code, credentials, and files stay between your device and your hosts.

  • No analytics, no tracking.
  • No account with us. Tokens for your hosts and agents are kept in the iOS Keychain on your device.
  • Host list and preferences are stored on your device.
